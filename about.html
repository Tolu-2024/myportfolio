Hello, I'm Toluwanimi

I’m an SS1 student and web designer with a strong passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. I started my journey in web design out of curiosity, and since then, I’ve built real websites, learned to solve design problems, and improved my coding skills day by day.

What I enjoy most about web design is the ability to bring ideas to life using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I love the creative side of choosing layouts, colors, and animations—but I also care about how users experience the site. That’s why I’ve started learning about UI/UX design principles to make sure every website I build is easy to use and visually appealing.

I believe in learning by doing. So far, I’ve built a school website (www.lascon.com.ng), and I’m currently working on my second project. I plan to keep improving my skills, earn professional certifications, and help businesses, schools, and NGOs build an online presence.

Skills:

  • HTML5 and Semantic Markup
  • CSS3 and Responsive Layouts
  • JavaScript
  • UI/UX Design (Adobe XD, Figma)
  • Version Control (GitHub)

Outside of coding, I enjoy learning new things, helping others, and exploring technology. My dream is to become a professional web developer and software engineer, and I’m working step by step toward that goal.
